Typing Test Instructions

Welcome to TypeRacer — a simple typing speed test.

  • Select a difficulty level from the dropdown.
  • Start typing in the box to begin the test (timer starts on the first keystroke).
  • Correctly typed words will be highlighted in blue; mistakes will be highlighted in red.
  • Press Enter to finish the test and see your time and WPM.
  • Use Retry to clear the input and try again.

Good luck — focus on accuracy first, then speed.
